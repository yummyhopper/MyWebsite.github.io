About Max Orenstein

Hello! I'm Max Orenstein, a dedicated and versatile Communication: Data & Network Science major at the University of Pennsylvania, set to graduate in May 2025. With a solid foundation in data science, analytics, and philosophy, I've always been intrigued by the power of data and technology to shape our understanding and interaction with the world. My journey in the realm of data and analytics began at Germantown Friends School, where my fascination with statistics and programming took root. Serving as the captain of both the varsity soccer and wrestling teams, I learned the importance of leadership, teamwork, and perseverance early on. These lessons have stayed with me, guiding my academic and professional endeavors. Professionally, I've had the opportunity to contribute to the Mack Institute for Innovation Management as a Research Assistant. Here, I've embraced the challenge of analyzing global data to uncover innovation trends within Fortune 500 companies. Whether it's managing data extraction processes, utilizing the OpenAI API for text classification, or authoring research reports, I've developed a keen eye for detail and a knack for presenting complex information in an accessible manner. My role at SlightEdge Advertising Services further honed my adaptability and results-oriented mindset, leading B2C campaigns and mentoring new associates. And, as a Group Sales Intern with The Philadelphia Union, I dived into the world of digital marketing and team collaboration, initiating campaigns that spoke directly to the fans' hearts. Beyond the professional realm, I've committed myself to leadership and community service. As the treasurer of Sigma Alpha Epsilon, managing significant budgets and fostering financial responsibility, and mentoring at Beat The Streets Philadelphia, I've realized the impact of guiding and supporting others. Outside of work and academics, I'm an enthusiast of fitness, soccer, chess, and reading. My curiosity drives me to explore different cultures through travel and to seek new adventures, whether it's on the soccer field, across the chessboard, or through the pages of a book. I believe in the power of data to inform, innovate, and inspire. Let's connect and explore how we can make meaningful impacts together.
